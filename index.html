I am currently a Ph.D Candidate at Craft & Hawkins Department of Petroleum Engineering, Louisiana State University. In 2012, I received B.S. degree at China University of Petroleum, Beijing, and M.S. degree in 2014 at Department of Petroleum Engineering, University of Louisiana at Lafayette. During my doctoral study, I spent three summers at Pegasus Vertex as a research intern.

I am currently actively seeking a full-time Petroleum Engineer position in industry.

Research Interests

  • Drilling and Completion Applications with foam in Underbalanced drilling, Sand cleanout, Foam-assisted mud cap drilling, Foam cementing in low fracture formation
  • Production Nodal analysis, Multiphase flow, Foam-assisted liquid unloading
  • Machine Learning Neural networks, Regression, Classification, Supervised learning
